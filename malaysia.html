Malaysia


Greet elderly as uncle or aunty or 'pak cik' and 'mak cik' in Malay language

Always bring some gift or food if you are visiting someone's house, it shows respect and gratitude as guests

Always use right hand when eating and handling foods

Ask if anyone is going to eat the last piece of food before finishing it

If you want to offer something, please offer more than once because Malaysian tend to decline the first offer although they want it

Use hand gesture as a sign of thank you when vehicle slow down for you to cross the road
